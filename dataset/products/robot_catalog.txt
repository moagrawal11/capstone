PRODUCT: AX-100 Industrial Assembly Robot
=============================================================
Model: AX-100
Category: Industrial Automation
Release Year: 2023
Price: $48,500 (base configuration)
Overview:
The AX-100 is a 6-axis articulated industrial robot designed for high-speed assembly tasks on
manufacturing lines. It delivers precision positioning with a repeatability of ±0.02 mm, making it ideal for
electronics assembly, automotive parts fitting, and small component handling.
Key Specifications:
 Payload Capacity: 10 kg
 Reach: 1,450 mm
 Degrees of Freedom: 6
 Repeatability: ±0.02 mm
 Maximum Speed: 8,000 mm/s (TCP)
 Weight: 165 kg
 Power Consumption: 1.2 kW (idle), 3.5 kW (peak)
 IP Rating: IP54
 Mounting: Floor, ceiling, wall
 Controller: AX-Controller v3.2
 Communication Protocols: EtherCAT, Profinet, Modbus TCP, ROS2
Supported Operating Systems:
 AX-OS 4.0 (proprietary)
 ROS2 Humble (via bridge adapter)
Accessories:
 AX-GRP-100: Parallel jaw gripper (0–80 mm stroke)
 AX-CAM-3D: Integrated 3D vision system
 AX-FORCE: 6-axis force/torque sensor
 AX-TRACK: Linear track extension (2 m, 4 m, 6 m options)
Use Cases:


 PCB component placement
 Screw driving and fastening
 Pick-and-place from conveyor belts
 Quality inspection (with vision kit)
Certifications:
CE, UL, ISO 10218-1, RoHS

PRODUCT: AX-200 Collaborative Robot (Cobot)
=============================================================
Model: AX-200
Category: Collaborative Robot
Release Year: 2024
Price: $32,000 (base configuration)
Overview:
The AX-200 is a lightweight collaborative robot (cobot) designed to work safely alongside human
workers. Equipped with built-in force-torque sensing on all joints, it automatically stops when
unexpected contact is detected. Its tool-free quick-change end-effector system allows operators to
switch tasks in under 60 seconds.
Key Specifications:
 Payload Capacity: 5 kg
 Reach: 900 mm
 Degrees of Freedom: 6
 Repeatability: ±0.05 mm
 Maximum Speed: 1,500 mm/s (TCP)
 Weight: 18.4 kg
 Power Consumption: 150 W (idle), 500 W (peak)
 IP Rating: IP66
 Mounting: Floor, ceiling, wall, mobile base
 Controller: Embedded (tablet-based teach pendant)
 Communication Protocols: Modbus TCP, EtherNet/IP, USB, ROS2
Safety Features:
 Power and Force Limiting (PFL) — ISO/TS 15066 compliant
 Joint torque sensors on all 6 axes
 Configurable safety zones (speed, force, zone-based)
 Emergency stop (hardware + software)


Programming:
 Drag-and-drop visual programming (no-code interface)
 Python SDK (axiom-cobot-sdk >= 2.0)
 ROS2 Humble compatible
Accessories:
 AX-GRIP-MAG: Magnetic gripper (up to 3 kg)
 AX-GRIP-VAC: Vacuum gripper kit
 AX-WELD: MIG welding torch adapter
 AX-MOBILE-BASE: AGV mobile base integration kit
Use Cases:
 Machine tending

 Lab automation
 Packaging and palletizing
Certifications:
CE, UL, ISO/TS 15066, ISO 10218-1/2

PRODUCT: RX-500 Autonomous Mobile Robot (AMR)
=============================================================
Model: RX-500
Category: Autonomous Mobile Robot
Release Year: 2023
Price: $21,000
Overview:

localization and mapping (SLAM) to navigate without floor markings or QR codes, adapting dynamically
to changing environments. It integrates with warehouse management systems (WMS) via REST API.
Key Specifications:
 Maximum Payload: 250 kg (flatbed) / 100 kg (shelf-tote)
 Maximum Speed: 2.0 m/s (loaded), 2.5 m/s (unloaded)
 Battery: 48 V LiFePO4, 100 Ah
 Battery Life: 10–12 hours per charge


 Charging: Auto-docking, 2-hour fast charge (20%–100%)
 Navigation: SLAM (LiDAR + depth cameras)
 Safety Sensors: 2× LiDAR (360°), 4× ultrasonic, 2× depth cameras
 Communication: Wi-Fi 6 (802.11ax), optional 4G/LTE
 Fleet Management: Axiom Fleet Manager (cloud or on-premise)
 IP Rating: IP52
 Operating Temperature: 0°C to 45°C
Supported WMS Integrations:
 SAP EWM
 Oracle WMS Cloud
 Manhattan Associates WMS
 Custom REST API
Use Cases:
 Goods-to-person fulfillment
 Inter-department material transport
 Hospital medication and supply delivery
 Cleanroom logistics
Certifications:
CE, UL, ISO 3691-4, IEC 62133

PRODUCT: EX-300 Exoskeleton — Industrial Assist
=============================================================
Model: EX-300
Category: Wearable Robotics / Exoskeleton
Release Year: 2024
Price: $9,800 per unit
Overview:
The EX-300 is a passive-active hybrid exoskeleton designed to reduce musculoskeletal strain for
workers performing repetitive overhead or heavy-lifting tasks. It provides up to 20 kg of lift assistance
and monitors the wearer's biomechanics in real-time through embedded IMU sensors.
Key Specifications:


 Assist Force: Up to 20 kg equivalent lift support
 Weight (unit): 6.8 kg
 Degrees of Assisted Motion: Shoulder (2-DOF), lower back (1-DOF)
 Battery: 7.4 V, 10 Ah; 8-hour runtime
 Sensors: 9-axis IMU per joint, pressure insoles (optional)
 Data Output: Bluetooth 5.0 to Axiom ErgoApp
 Adjustable Fit: S/M/L/XL frame sizes (torso and leg straps)
 Operating Temperature: -10°C to 50°C
Software:
 Axiom ErgoApp (iOS / Android / Windows)
 Dashboards for team fatigue monitoring
 Alert thresholds for overexertion events
 CSV data export for EHS reporting
Use Cases:
 Warehouse picking and packing
 Automotive assembly line overhead tasks
 Construction structural assembly
 Aircraft maintenance
Certifications:
CE, ISO 16570, OSHA compliant guidelines
