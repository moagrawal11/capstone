SECTION 2: WARRANTY CLAIM PROCESS
=============================================================
2.1 How to File a Warranty Claim
Step 1 — Register your product (if not already done):
Visit register.axiomrobotics.com and complete product registration using your serial number and
purchase order number. Registration is required to activate warranty services.
Step 2 — Contact Axiom Support:
 Submit a ticket at support.axiomrobotics.com, OR
 Call +1-800-AXIOM-RX during business hours (8 AM–6 PM EST, weekdays), OR
 Email warranty@axiomrobotics.com with subject line "Warranty Claim — [Model] — [Serial Number]"
Step 3 — Provide the following information:
 Product model and serial number
 Date of purchase and proof of purchase (invoice or order confirmation)
 Description of the defect and steps to reproduce
 Fault codes or error logs (exported from AX-OS or Fleet Manager)
 Photos or video of the defect (where applicable)
Step 4 — Axiom Support Review:
Axiom will acknowledge your claim within 1 business day and may request additional information. A
warranty determination (approved or denied) will be issued within 5 business days of receiving all
required information.
2.2 Warranty Resolution Options
If your claim is approved, Axiom will choose the most appropriate remedy:
A. On-site repair: An Axiom certified technician will be dispatched to your site (standard for AX-100,
RX-500, and large components).
B. Advance replacement: A replacement unit or part will be shipped to you before you return the
defective item (advance credit card hold required for unit replacements; released upon return of
defective unit within 30 days).
C. Depot repair: You ship the unit to an Axiom service center; repaired and returned within 10 business
days of receipt. Axiom covers inbound and outbound shipping costs for valid warranty claims.
D. Replacement unit: For products under 90 days old with a confirmed manufacturing defect, Axiom will
issue a full unit replacement.
2.3 Out-of-Warranty Repairs
Non-warranty repairs are available at Axiom's standard service rates:
 Diagnostic fee: $250 (applied toward repair cost if approved)
 Labor rate: $185/hour
 Parts: Cost + 15% handling
 Travel fee for on-site service: $0.65/mile + $150 dispatch fee
Contact service@axiomrobotics.com for a repair quotation.

SECTION 6: PREVENTIVE MAINTENANCE SCHEDULE
=============================================================
Following the recommended maintenance schedule is a condition for warranty coverage.
Failure to maintain service records may void warranty for related component failures.
AX-100 Industrial Robot — Preventive Maintenance Schedule:
Monthly:
 Inspect robot for loose fasteners, cable wear, gripper wear
 Clean all vents and cooling fans
 Review fault log and clear non-critical entries
Every 3 months:
 Lubricate joint 1-3 gearboxes per lubrication chart (MM-AX100-EN §7)


 Inspect and torque all external fasteners to spec
 Clean and inspect teaching pendant and cable
Every 6 months:
 Full joint calibration (AX-OS → Diagnostics → Calibration)
 Inspect brake function on all joints
 Check encoder cable insulation
Annually:
 Full overhaul inspection by Axiom certified technician (required to maintain extended warranty)
 Replace worn seals and belts per service bulletin
RX-500 AMR — Preventive Maintenance Schedule:
Weekly:
 Clean LiDAR sensor windows (dry microfiber cloth)
 Clean charging contacts (dry cloth)
 Review battery health in Fleet Manager
Monthly:
 Inspect drive wheels for wear (replace if tread depth < 3 mm)
 Check all cable connections at maintenance access panel
 Verify map accuracy against physical environment
Every 6 months:
 Replace in-line air filter (if dusty environment)
 Inspect suspension components
 Full diagnostic report review
Annually:
 Battery capacity test and cell inspection
 Drive motor inspection
 Certified technician inspection
EX-300 Exoskeleton — Preventive Maintenance Schedule:
Daily:
 Inspect harness straps for fraying or buckle damage
 Wipe down frame with clean, damp cloth
Monthly:
 Check joint pivot points for smooth operation — apply EX-LUBE-KIT if stiff
 Inspect battery connector pins for corrosion
 Review ErgoApp session reports for anomalies
Every 6 months:
 Replace harness strap set (EX-STRAP-KIT) — mandatory per safety guidelines if visible wear
 Full inspection by supervisory safety officer
 Battery capacity test and replacement if below 80% of original capacity
